Stop word lists

Here is a list of stop words that have been compiled through the WWW. Some of these are public, some are not. Please make sure that the private ones are not leaked outside rpnlpir / SoC. Thanks!

The files are organized in the original source form and then a version that is processed into a "word per line" form (which is the .txt file).

N.B. currently (Wed May 28 10:50:48 GMT-8 2003) these files are all for the English language.

  • DVL/Verity stop list. From Verity corp, modified by dVL. Found on the web at http://dvl.dtic.mil/stop_list.pdf on Wed May 28 10:38:02 GMT-8 2003. [private, filename: verity]
  • SearchEngineWorld's Keyword Surveillance Tool (KST). Found on the web at http://www.searchengineworld.com/spy/stopwords.htm [private, filename: searchEngineWorld]
  • A stoplist from DCS, Glasgow, UK. Probably from the van Rijsbergen book. [public, filename: dcs-gla-ac-uk]
  • A stoplist from versuslaw.com. Saved from the web at http://www.versuslaw.com/help/search/printablestop.asp. Probably proprietary. [private, filename: versuslaw]

It is relatively easy to construct your own stop word list. You can either take the approach of stopping high frequency words or closed-class words. Either way gives you a candidate stop list to start from.
